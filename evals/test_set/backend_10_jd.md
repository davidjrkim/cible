# Senior Backend Engineer — Render

**Company:** Render
**Location:** Remote (North America)

## About Render

Render is the unified cloud for hosting apps and databases. We compete with Heroku for the "just deploy my code" segment. We run a fleet of customer workloads on top of Kubernetes across multiple regions.

## What you'll do

- Work on the customer-facing build + deploy pipeline (Go services, customer build runners in containers)
- Improve the autoscaler for customer web services
- Own a chunk of the managed Postgres product (provisioning, backups, PITR, failover)
- On-call rotation, weekly

## Requirements

- 5+ years backend experience
- Strong Go
- Comfort with Kubernetes as an operator — you've debugged a stuck deployment, not just used `kubectl get pods`
- You've built or operated multi-tenant infrastructure (security boundaries between customers actually mattered to your previous job)

## Bonus

- Postgres operations experience (PITR, logical replication, failover automation)
- Build-system or compiler-cache experience (we care a lot about build performance)
