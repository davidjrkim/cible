# Full-Stack Engineer — Cal.com

**Company:** Cal.com
**Location:** Fully remote (we're a remote-first OSS company)

## What we do

Cal.com is the open-source Calendly alternative used by tens of thousands of teams. Everything we ship is on GitHub, and our community contributes a meaningful share of every release.

## What you'll do

- Build features end-to-end across our Next.js + Prisma + Postgres stack
- Triage and merge community PRs (this is real work, not a side task)
- Own at least one of: scheduling engine, team availability, payments, or apps marketplace
- Write public RFC documents before doing nontrivial work — discussion happens in the open

## What we look for

- 3+ years production experience with TypeScript and React
- You've worked with Prisma or another full TypeScript ORM and know its sharp edges
- Comfort with date / timezone math (you've debugged a DST transition bug)
- You've maintained an open source project, or contributed substantially to one

## Bonus

- Experience with Stripe Connect or marketplace payment flows
- You can write product copy that doesn't sound like marketing
