# Senior Product Designer — Figma

**Company:** Figma
**Location:** Remote (US/Canada) or NYC
**Team:** FigJam

## About the role

You'd be a senior product designer on FigJam — Figma's collaborative whiteboarding product. Specifically, you'd own the AI-features surface: assist tools that turn rough sketches into structured diagrams, summarize sticky-note walls, etc.

## What you'd do

- Own the design of new AI features end-to-end: from user research, to interaction model, to ship
- Partner with two engineers and a PM in a small pod that ships weekly
- Run user research sessions yourself — we don't have a separate research team between you and your users
- Write public Figma blog posts about your work; this surface is part of how Figma communicates externally

## Requirements

- 5+ years product design at consumer or SaaS scale
- A portfolio showing actual shipped work, not concept pieces
- Experience designing for AI-driven interfaces — you have a point of view about loading states, model errors, trust, and reversibility
- You can prototype interactively (you don't need to ship production code, but you can build a Framer prototype to test an interaction model)

## Bonus

- You've done qualitative user research as a primary part of your job
- You write well — blog posts, internal RFCs, hallway-conversation summaries
