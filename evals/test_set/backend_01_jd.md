# Senior Backend Engineer, Data Platform — Ramp

**Company:** Ramp
**Location:** New York City (hybrid, 3 days in office)
**Team:** Data Platform

## About the role

Ramp is the corporate card and spend management platform used by 25,000+ companies. The Data Platform team owns the pipelines that move tens of millions of card transactions per day from card networks to our ledger, our risk models, and our customer-facing analytics.

You will own backend services in Go and Python, design event-driven pipelines on Kafka and Flink, and work closely with our risk and ML teams to make sure their data arrives correct and on time.

## Responsibilities

- Design and operate high-throughput data ingestion pipelines (Kafka, Flink, Postgres, Snowflake)
- Improve the reliability of our exactly-once delivery semantics across the ingestion path
- Reduce p99 ingestion latency from card network webhook → ledger row
- Partner with risk and ML teams on feature freshness and backfills

## What we look for

- 5+ years backend experience, ideally in fintech, ads, or another high-throughput domain
- Strong Go OR Python production experience
- Experience with at least one of: Kafka, Kinesis, Pulsar
- You've operated a stateful streaming system (Flink, Spark Streaming, Beam) in production
- You can debug a distributed pipeline from a Grafana dashboard at 2am

## Nice to have

- Postgres internals (replication slots, logical decoding)
- Exposure to financial ledger systems and double-entry accounting

## Compensation

$220k–$280k base + equity, NYC.
