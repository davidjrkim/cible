# Backend Engineer — Stripe (Payments Reliability)

**Company:** Stripe
**Location:** Dublin or Remote (EU)
**Team:** Payments Reliability

## What this team does

Payments Reliability owns the parts of Stripe that route, retry, and recover failed payments. When a card issuer drops a connection or returns a soft decline, this team's code decides whether to retry, when, and how.

## What you'll do

- Build and operate services in Ruby (Stripe's main backend language) and Scala (some downstream pipelines)
- Improve retry logic — every percentage point of recovered authorizations is real revenue for our customers
- Design protocols for talking to legacy bank-network APIs (ISO 8583 is still around — sorry)
- Carry a pager. We move fast in production and we own what we ship.

## What we need

- 4+ years backend engineering
- You've built or operated a service handling >1k req/s
- Comfort with at least one statically- or dynamically-typed backend language; willingness to learn Ruby quickly if you don't have it
- You think about p99 latencies and tail behavior, not just averages

## Bonus

- Payments / fintech background
- ISO 8583 or similar batch-mode-from-the-90s protocol experience
