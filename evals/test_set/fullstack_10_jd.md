We're building an AI-native legal research product for in-house counsel at mid-market companies. Two technical founders ex-OpenAI, two ex-BigLaw advisors. Raised $4M from a16z and Sequoia.

You'd be the third engineer. That means:

- Move fast. We've shipped 6 versions of the product in 4 months and intend to keep that pace

- 3+ years production engineering experience
- You've shipped at least one feature that touched an LLM API end-to-end (not just a demo)
- You're comfortable touching the model layer (eval-driven prompt iteration, structured outputs, tool use) and the application layer (Next.js, Postgres, deployment)
