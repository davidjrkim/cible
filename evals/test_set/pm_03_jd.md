# Product Manager — Linear (Insights)

**Company:** Linear
**Location:** Remote (Europe / Americas)
**Team:** Insights

## About the role

You'd be the second PM at Linear, owning the new Insights surface — dashboards, metrics, and reports that help engineering leaders see what their teams are shipping (or not).

## What you'd do

- Own the Insights product end-to-end: roadmap, customer interviews, written specs, ship/no-ship calls
- Work in a 5-person pod (2 engineers, 1 designer, you, plus a part-time founder-as-PM)
- Lead 2-3 customer interviews per week — with engineering managers and VPs of Engineering, mostly
- Write opinionated specs. Linear's culture is "have a strong point of view, then revise it when you're shown wrong."

## Requirements

- 4+ years of product management
- You've shipped a B2B SaaS product to engineering or developer customers
- You write well — specs, customer call notes, internal RFCs
- You can read SQL and reason about what a dashboard query is actually doing

## Bonus

- You've worked on an analytics or BI product before
- You're a power user of Linear (we'll notice)
