# Senior Backend Engineer — Sentry

**Company:** Sentry
**Location:** Remote (US/Europe)
**Team:** Ingest

## About the team

Ingest is the team that takes the firehose of error events, traces, and replays from customer SDKs and lands them durably in our processing pipeline. We process tens of billions of events per day.

## What you'd do

- Work on our Kafka-backed event ingestion pipeline (Python + Rust + a little Go)
- Improve rate-limiting and quota enforcement (this is a queue + cardinality control problem more than a CPU one)
- Help land our rewrite of the symbolicator service (Rust)
- On-call rotation, weekly

## Requirements

- 5+ years backend
- You've operated a streaming pipeline at meaningful scale — Kafka, Pulsar, Kinesis, anything
- Comfortable in Python; willingness to do real work in Rust if you don't have it yet
- You understand back-pressure, head-of-line blocking, and quota fairness as more than vocabulary

## Bonus

- Sentry / Datadog / NewRelic / observability product background
- You've debugged a runaway-cardinality bug in production
