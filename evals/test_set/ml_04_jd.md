# ML Research Engineer — Mistral

**Company:** Mistral AI
**Location:** Paris (3 days in office)
**Team:** Pretraining

## About the role

You'd join the pretraining team at Mistral. We train frontier open-weights and proprietary models. Your work is upstream of every downstream product — small wins here compound massively.

## What you'd do

- Improve our data pipeline: filtering, deduplication, quality scoring, mixture experimentation
- Run pretraining experiments at the small-scale and validate scaling behavior
- Work on architectural changes (mixture-of-experts routing, attention variants, etc.)
- Write internal papers documenting findings

## Requirements

- 4+ years of ML engineering or ML research
- You've trained a transformer model from scratch at meaningful scale (>1B params)
- Strong PyTorch; comfortable in distributed training (FSDP / DeepSpeed / Megatron / TP+PP)
- You read recent ML papers and can implement them from the paper alone

## Bonus

- You've authored or co-authored a paper on a related topic
- Data-pipeline / data-quality experience for LLM pretraining
