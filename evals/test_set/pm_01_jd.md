# Senior Product Manager, Growth — Vercel

**Company:** Vercel
**Location:** Remote (US / EU)
**Team:** Growth

## About the role

Vercel is the platform for frontend developers. We power Next.js, host millions of sites, and ship infrastructure that lets a solo developer compete with a team.

Growth at Vercel is not "vanity metrics" growth. We measure: time-from-signup-to-first-deploy, free-to-paid conversion on real workloads, expansion within an account. The Growth PM owns the funnel from a developer's first `next dev` to their team's first paid invoice.

## Responsibilities

- Own a portion of the activation, conversion, or expansion funnel
- Define the metric, write the brief, prioritize the experiments
- Work alongside a 4-person pod (eng + design + data + you)
- Run rigorous experiments — we use a real stats framework, no `p < 0.1` heroics
- Partner with DevRel and Marketing on launches that actually move metrics, not just inbox impressions

## What we look for

- 4+ years PM experience, with 2+ on growth, activation, or monetization specifically
- Technical comfort: you can read a Next.js project, write a SQL query, and reason about CDN invalidation
- Strong opinions about experimentation. You know what "novelty effect" means and why pre-registering matters.
- You've shipped at least one experiment that delivered a sustained, statistically credible win
- Comfort with data tools: SQL is mandatory; dbt or Mode is nice

## What we don't look for

- Roadmap-curators
- PMs who outsource the SQL

## Compensation

Competitive. Remote-first.
