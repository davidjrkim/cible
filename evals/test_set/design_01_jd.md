# Product Designer — Arc Browser (The Browser Company)

**Company:** The Browser Company
**Location:** New York City (hybrid)
**Team:** Product Design

## About us

We're building Arc — a browser that thinks of you as a person, not a tab count. Our customers are creators, founders, students, and curious people who care how their software feels.

Design at The Browser Company is not decoration. We ship the design. Our designers prototype in code, write product specs, and own features end-to-end alongside engineers.

## What you'll do

- Own the design of one product surface (sidebar, command bar, spaces, or a new surface entirely)
- Prototype in code (SwiftUI, React, or Figma → working prototype with our prototyping team)
- Write the spec that engineers build from
- Partner with research to test prototypes on real users in our weekly research cadence
- Defend opinionated, polished, sometimes weird design choices

## What we look for

- 5+ years designing consumer software with real users
- A portfolio with at least one launched product you are proud of
- You can write — specs, rationale, marketing copy when needed
- Comfort with prototyping in code, or strong desire to learn
- You sweat the details: motion, typography, copy, sound

## What we don't look for

- Process for process's sake
- "Design systems specialists" with no shipping experience
- Anyone who thinks design is wireframes

## Compensation

$160k–$200k + equity. NYC, hybrid 2 days/week.
